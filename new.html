1. Overview

.

The following projects were done at NewDealDesign in the last 2 years. During this time I led the Experience Design team, playing a key role in the direction for interaction design on both physical and digital projects, merging my background in digital products with my studies in interaction design and my passion for human psychology.

2. Physical UX

Postmates

Autonomous Delivery Robot
Industrial and Experience Design for Serve.

.

Contributions

Interaction architecture, Interface design language, Prototyping, Experience concepting.

.

Roles

Myself: Lead Experience Designer
Team: Erik Askin, Ben Wong, Alex Destasio, Maiya Jensen, Jay Murallon

.

A 6 month sprint to a working prototype, it was important to tie in human empathy into the physical design of the robot to address the harsh view of robots on the streets. Working closely with the industrial design team, I helped shape the interaction architecture and contributed design concepts focused on building a personality for Serve.

.

.

We developed the foundation for interaction on the concept of 3 levels, 1ft for customers and pedestrians, 5ft for passing foot traffic, and 30ft for vehicles. We worked on building the visual communication system for navigating, customer facing interactions, and public street facing interactions.

5. Summary

.

Thank you for taking the time to look through my work! My time at NewDealDesign has allowed me to pursue my interests in designing new interactions and bringing personality to interfaces. In my time here I have learned valuable collaboration skills with Industrial Designers, Strategists and engineers. Most importantly I've learned how to work fast and be bold. I would love to continue to grow in my craft and to take a slower pace, develop my point of view, and learn from more talented designers. Thank you!

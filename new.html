1. Overview

.

Over the past three years at NewDealDesign I have worked on more than 30 projects with companies like Ford, Postmates, T-Mobile, LG, and Lenovo. During this time I led the Experience Design team, building the interaction design practice in both physical and digital projects. In my work I merge my background in digital products with my studies in interaction design and my passion for human psychology.

.

Hop On Hop Off Urban Transport

Designing an open and hygienic transport.

.

.

.

Rolla is a mass-transit alternative system combing a hop on, hop off, low-speed autonomous electric vehicle with a ride-hailing App and central server, together acting as an alternative mass- transit for urban centers.

.

.

.

As a system, Rolla can run pre-programmed routes to augment public transportation, be called on demand through the ride-hailing app, or function as a branded experience allowing local business to have Rolla bring patrons to their doorstep.

5. Summary

.

My work focuses on humanizing technology. Our interactions with autonomous vehicles, robots, interfaces, smart systems, can take cues from our history as humans. Often I search for a human mental model, or a unique micro interaction that makes an engagement warmer or surprising. My time at NewDealDesign has allowed me to pursue this interest in designing new interactions and bringing personality to interfaces. I continue to learn new ways to bring technology experiences to life and look forward to the work ahead.
